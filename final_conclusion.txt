[KEY STATISTICAL FINDINGS]
- Dataset: YRBS 2007 (N = 13,174)
- Sad/Hopeless Group Smoking Rate: 27.61%
- Comparison Group Smoking Rate: 16.18%
- Net Difference: +11.43%
- Statistical Inference: Z = 15.0536, P = 3.27e-51 (Reject H0)
- 95% Confidence Interval: [9.83%, 13.02%]

[RISK ESCALATOR & EXTENDED ANALYSIS]
Exploratory analysis reveals a significant "Risk Escalator Effect":
1. Comorbidity Impact: Students facing both sadness and binge drinking 
   exhibit a smoking prevalence of 54.8%.
2. Psychological Beacon: 79.9% of adolescent smokers demonstrate 
   underlying psychological distress or substance abuse. Smoking is a 
   critical "radar beacon" for invisible trauma rather than just 
   disciplinary misconduct.

[DISCLAIMER]
This study is based on cross-sectional observational data. Results 
confirm a robust statistical association but do not imply causality.

- 對照組吸菸率 : 16.18%

[延伸洞察：風險階梯效應]
根據探索性分析(EDA)，吸菸行為呈現顯著的共病風險階梯：
1. 當悲傷情緒與狂飲行為(Binge Drinking)同時出現時，
   吸菸率急遽飆升至 54.8%。
2. 數據解剖顯示，79.9% 的吸菸青少年皆伴隨心理困擾或酒精濫用。
3. 吸菸行為已非單純紀律問題，而是心理創傷的「雷達信標」。

[聲明]
本研究為觀察性研究(Observational Study)，所得結論僅證實
高度統計相關性(Association)，並不代表因果關係(Causality)。
